About Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty, also known as Kitty White, was born in the suburbs of London. She lives with her parents and her twin sister Mimmy. Hello Kitty is five apples tall and weighs three apples. She loves to bake cookies and make new friends.

First introduced by Sanrio in 1974, Hello Kitty has become a global phenomenon and one of the most successful marketing brands in the world. Despite her cat-like appearance, Hello Kitty is actually a little girl and not a cat!

Hello Kitty's full name is Kitty White. She was born on November 1st in the suburbs of London, England. Her height is five apples and her weight is three apples.
